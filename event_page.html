Events and shows

Events, meetings and other activities are very effective communication tools both at an internal and external level. They enable and foster interpersonal relationships. They improve motivation and working mood. They reward achievements and recognize success.Hunt down local, live music shows at restaurants on the weekend, find a YouTube concert video, or go to a student band concert. If your campus allows in-person sporting events, attend as many as you feel comfortable with. Attending them at your school, is fun and usually free.2
